Lemon

Words can't express my lemon
good thing that life gave it out.
Done more miles than Magellan
still sees me through - round about.

The door crushed me whe it slammed
but time's wheels must keep spinning.
Like your mixtape that's still jammed
I'm back to the beginning.

There's no sign of where to go
guess I'm along for the ride.
I'll just press down low and slow.
use a new light as my guide.

Nuclear Family

Family is a funny word
the concept is a bit absurd.
Paying back a debt incurred
with love that's better seen than heard.

Did I sign on family lines?
I always end up in these binds.
Forever too green on the vines
To understand these blood confines.

Economically speaking
with all the havoc that we're wreaking.
Is progeny worth seeking?
These thoughts keep my interest piquing.

Family's so nuclear
a phenomenon peculiar.
Rookie bioengineers
humanity's so cavalier.

Life wasn't on my wish list
Domestic bliss doesn't exist
Is there something that I missed
A reason for genes to persit.

Sorry dear old mom and dad
existing isn't all that bad
just think children were a fad
there's noone left for me to add.

Temporary Files

Oh gosh it's your birthday
why can't I forget?
My memory's waylaid
my conscious again.

I wrote you off,
unsubscribed,
and wiped your data clean.
Surely the thought's a GDPR breach.

If you were a file
I'd right-click delete.
Are you sure? it would ask me.
I wouldn't even blink.

Can't seem to delete you;
what am I to do?
Are you stuck in my head
til the day that I'm dead?

You're hogging my disc space
you utter disgrace.
Yes, you total utter disgrace!

Now I'll reboot and remove
those temporary files;
and corrupt your mixtape
that I've played for miles.

I'll upgrade my memories with somebody new.
Defragment my heart-drive
make my dreams come true.

If life is a network
don't connect to mine.
Unless you know what love is
I won't give a dime.

Last week was your birthday
and it slipped my mind
yes it totally slipped my mind.

Double Yellow Lines

They say life's like a highway
but then why am I so low.
Kept staring forward to one day
No two ever in a row.

Am I tresspassing through?
I've paid the trolls my fare.
The road to home it never queues
God don't divert me there.

These double yellow lines
they paint between your place and mine;
No stopping waiting or overtaking
allowed at any time.

So you just keep on to the left
and I'll keep to the right.
When breathing deep feels just like theft
love's never black and white.

Does a rose that grows so far away
even still have a smell?
They've levied me my Chevrolet
Now I drive a blue Vauxhall

Those lines are drawn with thick red tape
they blare: "you shall not pass!"
I dream of making our escape
until we run out of gas.

Those double yellow lines
Those double yellow lines
We'll have to learn to roll in our hay
while the sun still shines.

Those double yellow lines
Those double yellow lines
One day I hope to cut across
without incurring any fines
